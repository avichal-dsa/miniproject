I have taken my mini project as a Scientific Calculator which deals with 3 types of operations:-
1. Basic
2. Geometric
3. Advanced

The Basic Operations deals with the following simple operations of a basic calculator:-
1. Addition
2. Subtraction
3. Multiplication
4. Division
5. Exponential Operations

The Geometric Operation deals with the following operations:-
1. Trigonometric Calculations
2. Polar Coordinates Conversion
3. Area Volume
4. Decimal Conversion
5. Polynomial Equation Solving

The Advanced Operations deals with the following:-
1. Differentiation
2. Integeration
3. Differential Equations
4. Complex Numbers
5. Vectors
6. Statistics
7. Graphical
